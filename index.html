About Longitude

Dublin's largest outdoor music festival takes place every third weekend of July in Marlay Park.

Longitude has hosted loads of acts in the last five years including Phoenix, Vampire Weekend, Disclosure, Bastille, Kendrick Lamar, The National, and The Weeknd.
